Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

The BCCA Finance Dashboard is an internal tool for the Bellingham Co-Housing Association (BCCA) to view financial data from QuickBooks Online.

Data Collection

This application accesses the following data from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Balance Sheet reports
  • Profit and Loss reports
  • Transaction records

Data Usage

Financial data is used solely to generate dashboards and reports for BCCA members. Data is:

  • Stored securely in Azure Blob Storage
  • Displayed only on the BCCA Finance Dashboard
  • Not shared with any third parties
  • Not used for marketing or advertising

Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored securely and refreshed as needed. Financial data snapshots are retained for historical comparison.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the BCCA dashboard administrator.
